Our Process

Request for Verification

Clients submit a request for verification form.

Request Review

Risk, conflict of interest analysis, and internal capacity determination.

Verification Acceptance and Pack

Acceptance letter and verification methodology sent out to the client.

Verification Process

File submission and review (initial), on-site planning, on-site verification, and preliminary score.

Verification Conclusion and Issue

Final score, decision on verification, internal sign-off, and certification (final B-BBEE report).

WHat is AKani?

AKANI is a Tsonga word/name meaning Building together. Our mission therefore follows the same philosophy “Together WE Build”
